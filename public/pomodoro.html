The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method where you split up your work/study time with short breaks between intervals. Typically one pomodoro work period lasts for around 25 minutes, and is then followed by a short break, usually around 5 - 10 minutes. After 4 pomodoro work periods, take one long break, which usually takes around 15 - 20 minutes. Splitting up your time like this makes it less likely that you get burned out by your long work hours, and many people find the Pomodoro Technique useful for increasing productivity.
